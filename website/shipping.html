Shipping & Returns

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Shipping

  • Domestic (U.S.): Standard ground orders typically ship within 1–2 business days; express options may be available at checkout.
  • Oversized items: Bumpers, hoods, and glass may require freight delivery and additional handling time.
  • International: Duties, taxes, and customs clearance are the recipient’s responsibility unless stated otherwise.

Returns

  • Unused items in original packaging may be returned within 30 days of delivery, subject to inspection.
  • Electrical and programmed components may be non-returnable once installed or opened—check the product page for details.
  • Return shipping costs may apply unless the return is due to our error or a defective product covered by warranty.

Damaged shipments

Inspect packages on delivery. Report damage within 48 hours with photos of the packaging and part; we will coordinate a replacement or refund.

Contact support@autopartsglobal.com or use the form on our Contact page.
